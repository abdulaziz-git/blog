Oily Nose Treatment Plan



Oily noses can be caused by hormonal fluctuations, gender or by having an oily skin type. Here's how you can get rid of that oily nose.

If you suffer from an oilier nose then you probably already know what it feels like. An oily nose is usually the result of an excess amount of sebum production from the pores of the skin. Sebum is made up of oils and fats and is present under the outer layer of the skin.

Oily Nose Treatment Plan

Make sure that you are using noncomedogenic product because it is safe for your pores by not clogging it.

1. Cleanse your face at least twice a day, AM and PM routine

Create a skin routine to wash your face twice a day, in the morning and before you sleep at night to make sure no dirt, excess oils and makeup stick to your facial skin. Warm water and a gentle facial wash can do the job.

2. Use a hydrating moisturizer

The second step after washing your face is to use an oil-free moisturizer. This will help your nose area to keep hydrated and to send signal to the skin so that it doesn’t produce excess sebum. Oatmeal is one of the best ingredients to absorb excess oil on the skin.

3. Treat with salicylic acid

Salicylic acid is really good at unclog the skin pores, remove excess sebum and exfoliate the skin. Use during PM routine so the process will happen when you sleeping.

4. Exfoliate your face

Twice or three times in a week is enough so that it does not damage the skin barrier. Exfoliation can help removes dead skin after you treat it with salicylic acid. It is an important step to avoid accumulation of dead skin on the nose part so your skin is breathing properly.

5. Try a clay mask

Clay mask have the ability to draw out excess sebum from skin pores, especially the one with bentonite active ingredients. You can use clay mask once or twice a week.

Takeaway

Drink enough water everyday as to inform the skin that it is not in the dry mode. Get a healthy diet and limit eating spicy food. Spicy food can stimulate sebum production. If you need to use a makeup, avoid using oil-based makeup as it can make your skin shinier than usual since you have an oily skin.
